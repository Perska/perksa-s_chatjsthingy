SmileBASIC Source chatJS Plugins

Make life easier. Use plugins.

The chatJS Plugin System is a simple way to share your extensions and use others that people have shared to make your SmileBASIC Source chat experience better. This plugin system adds a simple way to load your favorite plugins by including a few lines in your chatJS, without hassle.

How it works

You just copy the contents of this file to your chatJS, refresh your chat, and you're ready! You can now use /loadplugin to load a new extra, or /localhelp to find out how to use a certain one.
